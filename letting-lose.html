Letting Lose

Leiden city center has lots of stuff to do with hundreds of little shops hidden between all the alleys. Leiden is also full of small parks, a few great bars and three movie theaters. Here is a basic list of how most people spend their time there.

  • Shopping on the Haarlemmerstraat, Breestraat and the Langegracht
  • Visiting local parks like Rembrandt or De Burcht
  • Going to the movies at lido, Trianon or kijkhuis
  • Visiting Museums like Hortus botanicus and Museum Volkenkunde
  • Going out for the night at bars like North End and Annie's
