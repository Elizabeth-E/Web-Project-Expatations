Elizabeth, Superwoman, Girl Genius or Best Web Designer Ever?

I was born on November 23, 1992 in a secret government test facility (hospital in Erie, Pennsylvania USA) created to become the hero of the technology world. Thus, why I’ve chosen to study I.T, it is my one true calling. However, my super powers are currently in the process of development. My boyfriend (the true Superman) inspired me to join the classes to help me develop faster and more efficiently. My strengths now aside from the ability to learn at inhuman speeds, are mainly coding in HTML, CSS and design. The most important quality of my work is that it defeats my arch nemesis, Captain Flaw, so my work must be perfect. By day I’m just the average student, by night I become a coding super genius and practice my powers to master them so that one day I can save the world from Captain Flaw’s minions, script kiddies. This site is my first attack against Captain Flaw. I hope you enjoy it and find it useful.
