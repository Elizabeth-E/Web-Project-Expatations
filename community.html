The Community

Leiden has an open and diverse community. For instance, there is a wide diversity of internationals here for school and work along with a large population of nationals. Many expats are coming from within Europe and the UK. Expats have created a sort of community of their own here in Leiden. All are very friendly and happy to help. No one is ever left on their own here. The Leiden expats even have their own Facebook page. Any expat in Leiden is always welcome and the group is so informative and friendly.

Feel free to also go check out the Leiden Expat Centre. They also have loads of information and are a great way to get started with integrating into the community.
