Do You Have Proper Identification?

  • a Dutch passport or a passport (or identity card) of a country belonging to the EU or the EEA.
  • Passports of all other countries must contain a valid residence sticker
  • a refugee travel document issued by the Dutch authorities
  • an alien’s travel document issued by the Dutch authorities
  • a residence permit or leave to remain card

Home Is Where The Heart Is

Most people that arrive in Leiden city center usually have a home in mind and have it already set up. If you are still looking I suggest contacting the school or employer that helped you get here. Many realtors are also very helpful (but sometimes pricey). Be careful if you choose to house hunt on your own. Expats are often a target for housing scams. If anything seems too good to be true it probably is. Watch out for any overly cheap places asking for anything more than 2 months’ rent as a deposit. Also steer clear if any bank transfers seem odd.
